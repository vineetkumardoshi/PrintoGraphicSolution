About Us

We are there for your needs. Always.

Printo Graphic Solution was established in 1994 by Shri Vinay Raijada. We, as a firm are committed towards providing quality printing and packaging solutions to corporates. For nearly 26 years, we have been pioneering printing and packaging solutions for small to large applications.

From mono cartons, tags, posters and danglers to leaflets, display boxes, corrugated boxes and more, our product portfolio is as diverse as it is ever-evolving. A robust and growing team makes our manufacturing services accessible across various demographics and geographies in the country.

We also embrace our responsibility towards the community through discouraging the use of plastic related polymers in our packaging.
